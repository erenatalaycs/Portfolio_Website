- **Room dimensions (D-01..D-03):** 4 m wide × 5 m deep × 2.6 m high. Concrete placements:
  - Back wall: BoxGeometry(4.0, 2.6, 0.05) — width = room width, height = room height, depth = wall thickness — positioned at `(0, 1.3, -2.525)`. Interior face exactly at z=-2.5.
  - Left wall: BoxGeometry(0.05, 2.6, 5.0) — positioned at `(-2.025, 1.3, 0)`. Interior face at x=-2.0.
  - Right wall: BoxGeometry(0.05, 2.6, 5.0) — positioned at `(2.025, 1.3, 0)`. Interior face at x=+2.0.
  - Ceiling: BoxGeometry(4.0, 0.05, 5.0) — positioned at `(0, 2.625, 0)`. Interior (underside) face at y=2.6.

- **Wall material (D-05):** `MeshStandardMaterial` matte, roughness 0.95, metalness 0, color SCENE_COLORS.wall (#0c0e12 from Plan 05-01). All three walls plus the ceiling receiveShadow true (the directional at (3, 4, 2) is the project's only shadow-caster).

- **Wall component shape (D-30):** Single Walls component renders all three walls inline as three mesh blocks. Subcomponents WallLeft / WallRight / WallBack are NOT broken out into separate exports — over-engineering for three meshes that share material. Component file header documents this discretion call.

- **Inward-facing orientation:** BoxGeometry is double-sided by default. The walls are thin (0.05 m); from inside the room, the interior face is visible. The camera cannot reach the exterior (maxDistance 2.6 from target (0, 0.85, 0)). No BackSide material trick needed.

- **Ceiling material:** Same as walls — SCENE_COLORS.wall, roughness 0.95, metalness 0, receiveShadow true. **Known minor visual oddity:** the directional light is at (3, 4, 2) — i.e. ABOVE the ceiling at y=2.6. From inside the room, the ceiling is lit only by the ambient + the recessed fixture (the directional cannot penetrate the opaque ceiling). This reads correctly as "lit from inside" — no retune of the directional in this plan. If the visual reads as wrong, surface in SUMMARY for a follow-up Plan 05-04 retune (move directional to e.g. (3, 2.4, 2) below the ceiling); do NOT block on it.

- **Ceiling fixture (D-20..D-22):**
  - Emissive disc: CylinderGeometry(0.12, 0.12, 0.02, 24) at position (0, 2.59, 0). The flat puck face points down into the room (default cylinder axis is Y — correct orientation). Material: MeshStandardMaterial with color SCENE_COLORS.wall (disc body invisible against ceiling; only emissive reads), emissive '#e8eef5' (cool white inline hex; not promoted to SCENE_COLORS — see note below), emissiveIntensity EMISSIVE_BUDGET.CEILING_FIXTURE (0.5 per Plan 05-01), toneMapped default true (D-25 reserves false for NEON_STRIP only).
  - PointLight: position (0, 2.55, 0), color '#d4dce4', intensity 0.3, distance 4.0, decay 2.0, castShadow false (D-21). Slightly below the disc so the light feels like it emanates downward into the room.

- **One-off fixture colors NOT promoted to SCENE_COLORS:** '#e8eef5' (emissive) and '#d4dce4' (light color) are inline hex literals in Ceiling.tsx with an inline comment ("Cool-white recessed-fixture tones. Inline because single-use — if a second cool-white emitter ever appears in v1.1+, promote both to SCENE_COLORS at that point."). This avoids polluting the palette with one-off scene-specific tones.
