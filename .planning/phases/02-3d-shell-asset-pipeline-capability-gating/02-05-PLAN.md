<objective>
Configure and enforce the three byte-budgets that Phase 2 ships: text shell entry < 120 KB gz, 3D lazy chunk < 450 KB gz, and a pre-configured-but-disabled GLB budget < 2500 KB that activates in Phase 4 when the workstation GLB lands. Wire the budgets via `size-limit`'s `package.json` config + a CI step in `.github/workflows/deploy.yml` that fails the build if any non-ignored budget is exceeded.

Purpose: Closes requirement OPS-02. The text-shell budget (120 KB gz) is the hard contract that justifies Phase 1's "recruiter ships in <2s" claim — if a future contributor lands a heavy dependency in the entry path, the build fails immediately and the regression cannot reach production. The 3D-chunk budget (450 KB gz) keeps R3F + drei + three from bloating beyond reasonable mid-tier-network expectations. The pre-configured GLB budget is a marker that Phase 4 can toggle ON by removing the `ignore: ['all']` line — no schema change needed.

Output: Updated `package.json` with the three size-limit entries + `size` and `size:why` scripts, updated `deploy.yml` with a CI step that runs `npx size-limit` after the build step, and recorded local measurements proving the budgets are PASSING at Plan 05 ship time. After this plan, the entire Phase 2 work is enforceable: every `git push origin main` triggers a build that catches text-shell bloat, 3D-chunk bloat, or future GLB bloat automatically.
</objective>
